Street Sweeping in Lancaster, PA

Interactive map of all 20 street sweeping routes in the City of Lancaster. Click any route to see its schedule. Use the location button to find your street.

Monday / Tuesday

Routes 1-4 sweep on the 1st & 3rd or 2nd & 4th Mon/Tue of each month.

Wednesday / Thursday

Routes 5-8 sweep on the 1st & 3rd or 2nd & 4th Wed/Thu of each month.

Special Routes

Alleys, night routes, business district (daily), development areas, and 5th-week routes on varying schedules.

Please visit the official City of Lancaster site for complete information on street cleaning.
