What it does

Three small things that add up to never getting another sweep ticket.

  1. 01

    Reads the parking sign for you.

    Take a photo of any street sweeping sign. The app extracts the days, hours, and posted exceptions automatically — no typing, no menus.

  2. 02

    Reminds you on time, the way you want.
